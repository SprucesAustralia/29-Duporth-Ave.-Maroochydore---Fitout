Scope of Work

Project Overview
Services Provided:
Initial Clean, Post-Defect Clean, Pre-Handover Clean
Location:
Ground Level (178.7 m²) and Level 1 (133.9 m²)
Total Area:
312.6 m²
Objective:
Prepare the site post-construction for handover by removing dust, debris, and light residues while ensuring safety and compliance.
Initial Preparation & Safety Compliance
Tasks:
Conduct a site inspection to identify hazards, access restrictions, and special requirements.
Ensure cleaning personnel are site-inducted, equipped with PPE, and compliant with Australian safety standards (e.g., NCC, local codes).
Confirm removal of major construction debris by the builder prior to cleaning.
Coordinate with other trades (e.g., flooring, painting) to ensure smooth workflow.
Tools:
Inspection checklist, PPE (gloves, safety glasses, vests), coordination logs
General Internal Areas
Tasks:
Remove loose dust, dirt, and residue from floors, walls, and ceilings.
Vacuum carpets; dust and mop tiled, vinyl, and timber floors to remove light stains and marks.
Clean doors, frames, handles, and stops (remove stickers/adhesives).
Wipe skirting boards, window sills, and ledges.
Dust signage, outlets, switches, fans, and air-conditioning vents.
Spot clean walls and surfaces for smudges and fingerprints.
Clean and vacuum internal stairwells, handrails, and balustrades.
Tools:
Vacuum cleaner, mop, bucket, cleaning cloths, duster, cleaning solutions
Windows & Glazing
Tasks:
Clean internal windows, frames, tracks, and sills; ensure smooth operation.
Remove protective tape, adhesive residue, and light marks from glass.
Wipe internal glazing panels, partitions, and mirrors.
Tools:
Glass cleaner, microfiber cloths, scraper for adhesive
Joinery & Fixtures
Tasks:
Dust and wipe joinery (cabinetry, shelves, cupboards – interior and exterior).
Clean fixtures (door handles, light switches, power outlets).
Clean basic kitchen surfaces, benchtops, and sinks (excludes deep appliance cleaning).
Tools:
Cleaning cloths, mild detergent, duster
Sanitary Areas (Bathrooms, Powder Rooms, Common Amenities)
Tasks:
Clean and disinfect toilets, urinals, sinks, and taps.
Wipe vanities, mirrors, towel rails, and soap holders.
Clean shower screens and remove protective stickers.
Mop and sanitize tiled and vinyl floors.
Tools:
Disinfectant, cleaning cloths, mop, glass cleaner, scraper
Balconies, Patios & External Areas
Tasks:
Sweep and remove loose dust and debris from balconies and outdoor areas.
Lightly wash flooring to remove dust and residue.
Ensure areas are free of general construction rubbish.
Tools:
Broom, hose, trash bags
Final Checks & Waste Disposal
Tasks:
Dispose of cleaning-related waste in designated bins.
Ensure the site is tidy and inspection-ready.
Note: No specialized treatments or deep polishing included.
Tools:
Trash bags, gloves
